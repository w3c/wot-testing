Fujitsu Limited

Fujitsu supports this specification, connecting the devices used in various fields and allowing them to be operated from cloud services.

A proxy and two devices were developed. The proxy aggregate devices connected to the local network and expose their shadows to the Internet for control by consumers. By blocking the local network from the Internet, a secure environment can be achieved without the device being accessed from the Internet. One device is a sensor and the other is an LED bulb. Both are Wi-Fi accessible and feature descriptions can be obtained during the discovery process.

Fujitsu also implements WoT in Virtuora MX providing Japanese market.
